About the Project Flowchart

The Project Flowchart was developed as a project to assist students in planning their courses throughout their academic career.

This tool provides a comprehensive view of course sequences, prerequisites, and corequisites, making it easier for students to navigate their educational path.

Based on Semester, the courses you pick will highlight other courses which are requisites to the class you want to see. The colour code is at the bottom left of the Flowchart section. You can use this as a reference during advising sessions with your counselor.

You can also use this to cross-reference what classes you will be enrolling in for your respective semester.
